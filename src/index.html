Azure Speech Demo

Azure Speech

Azure Speech Services are the unification of speech-to-text, text-to-speech, and speech-translation into a single Azure subscription.

Speech to Text

  • Transcribe short utterances.
  • Derive intents from results w/LUIS.
  • Batch transcription of audio files asynchronously.
  • Create and manage speech models.

Text To Speech

  • Standard voices
  • Neural voices
  • Convert text to speech.
  • Create and manage voice font deployments.

Speech Translation

  • Speech-to-text translation with recognition results.
  • Speech-to-speech translation.
  • Phone and email support
  • Interim recognition and translation results.
